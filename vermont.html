Northeast Adventures

Northeast Adventures

Fall 2017 brought about some interesting times... some friends from all different parts of the world came together when they never imagined it happening. A Bavarian, Korean-American, and a Native Vermonter. Jonathan (the German) and I (the Korean-American) met up at my home in New York and took a 6 hour bus ride up to Brattleboro, VT to see our good friend Simon (native Vermonter). Being away from the city really helps to refocus and bring life back into my soul, I feel as though sometimes being in the city drains me more than I realize until I get time away from it.

Such a blessing to meet these guys who I met in Norway!

We only stayed for 2 days but also got to go to one of the most hiked mountains in the world! We drove over to New Hampshire to do that hike and these are some of the reflections from my time with these 2 goons.
