Days with Islam

Days with Islam

Islam, is my friend, he is so hardworking and took me into his home in Baku, Azerbaijan. I'm honored to have him host me for 2 weeks while he is still working... such a busy guy!

Azerbaijan is not really like any other place I've been to, although before going I had done much research on Azerbaijan and the areas surrounding it. The research helps, but its much more impactful actually being there and talking to people there and living there. Being a part of life there. Hospitality is unmatched here.

I had originally come to work on a photography project here on the western border of Azerbaijan, the border that Armenia had overtaken as their own. I'm not sure why that was an interest to me but it just always caught my attention. As my research progressed, my interest became less in the actual conflict that the two countries had but more of seeing Azerbaijan as an outside. I was a complete foreigner in this place and fascinated. We did go as close to the border as we could, and interviewed some people in the area. The project was transforming, from being about a physical border to an unseen border that people had become complacent with.

sam.
