Heydar Aliyev Center/ Islam

Heydar Aliyev Center with Skagen


Being in Baku, Azerbaijan, you cannot go through the city without getting at least a small glimpse of the famous Heydar Aliyev center built by famed Iraqi-British architect Zaha Hadid.

So many things in Baku are named after their first secretary of Soviet Azerbaijan, Heydar Aliyev

I went over on one brisk morning with my friend and host, Islam, and we explored the building, photographing as we went. Skagen has a very clean and minimal look to their products so we wanted to express that through the similar aesthetic of the building. We wanted to have the piece be more interactive with the building itself so Islam would grab, jump, and walk on the sides of the curviture of the building.

The Center houses a conference hall, a gallery hall and a museum. The project is intended to play an integral role in the intellectual life of the city. Located close to the city center, the site plays a pivotal role in the redevelopment of Baku.
