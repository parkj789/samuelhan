"The Americans"

The Invisible Minority


Between 11 and 20-million undocumented immigrants live in the united states today, battling a national hardship over citizenship and immigration policy. Most are deeply embedded in our communities, and yet others may not realize how difficult life is for them. My project is to show these people the way they are. I will be focusing specifically on the Asian-American immigrants and a supreme court trial that will decide whether or not immigrants will be protected under deferred action for childhood arrivals (DACA) or not. the biggest part of my project will be done during the supreme court case of texas vs. united states on April 18th, 2016.
